Moonlight Bay is a popular song . It is commonly referred to as On Moonlight Bay . The music was written by Percy Wenrich , the lyrics by Edward Madden , and was published in 1912 . It was often sung in a Barbershop Quartet style , such as by Billy Murray and the American Quartet : The song was one of a number of early-20th-century songs which were used as titles of musical films made by Doris Day in the late 1940s and early 1950s .
