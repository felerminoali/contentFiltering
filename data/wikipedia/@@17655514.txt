She is the all-time top goalscorer of Iceland 's national team and competed at the UEFA Women 's Championships in 2009 and 2013 . In a club career sometimes disrupted by injury Margrt Lra has previously represented BV and Valur of the rvalsdeild , Duisburg and Turbine Potsdam of the German Bundesliga and Linkpings of the Damallsvenskan .
