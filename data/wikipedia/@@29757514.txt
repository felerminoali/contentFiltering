position = Goalkeeper Kim Wyant is an American Soccer player . She was the first Goalkeeper for the 1985 United States women 's national soccer team , appearing in the inaugural US game in Italy . She has played 16 international games as the US Goalkeeper , and is forever in the soccer history books of US Soccer for appearing as the first US goalkeeper and recording the first ever shutout , and first ever win ( July 1986 vs Canada ) . In 2008 , Kim was given the Special Recognition Award by the National Soccer Hall of Fame honoring her contribution to the winning way of the Womens National Team .
