The 2009 William &amp; Mary Tribe football team represented the College of William &amp; Mary in National Collegiate Athletic Association ( NCAA ) Division I Football Championship Subdivision ( FCS ) college football during the 2009 season . William &amp; Mary competed as a member of the Colonial Athletic Association ( CAA ) under head football coach Jimmye Laycock and played their home games at Zable Stadium . The 2009 campaign came on the heels of a 74 record in 2008 .
