The demonym for Campinas is a ' ' campineiro ' ' or campinense . # History # The city was founded on July 14 , 1774 , by Barreto Leme . It was initially a simple outpost on the way to Minas Gerais and Gois serving the Bandeirantes who were in search of precious minerals and Indian slaves . In the first half of the 19th century , Campinas became a growing population center , with many coffee , cotton and sugarcane farms . The construction of a railway linking the city of So Paulo to Santos ' seaport , in 1867 , was very important for its growth .
