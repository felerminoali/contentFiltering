' ' Annakodi ' ' is a 2013 Tamil film directed and written by Bharathiraja . It features Lakshman Narayan , Karthika Nair and Manoj Bharathiraja in the lead roles . The film has music by G. V. Prakash Kumar . The film was earlier titled ' ' Annakodiyum Kodiveeranum ' ' . # Cast # * Karthika Nair as Annakodi * Lakshman Narayan as Kodiveeran * Manoj Bharathiraja as Sadayan * Renuka * Meenal as Narthanga * Subiksha # Production # In August 2011 , Bharathiraja revealed that the film would be titled ' ' Annakodiyum Kodiveeranum ' ' and would have a village centric theme similar to films such as ' ' Paruthi Veeran ' ' and ' ' Subramaniyapuram ' ' . Parthiepan was signed on to play the title dual roles of father and son and subsequently completed a photo shoot for the film .
