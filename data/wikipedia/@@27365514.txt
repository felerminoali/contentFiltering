' ' Momay ' ' is a Filipino fantasy-drama television series which premiered May 24 , 2010 on ABS-CBN 's Primetime Bida block . The story revolves around a little girl who has died and come back as a ghost to protect and guard her family . # Story and Development # # Plot # Miley , also known by her family as Momay , was the daughter of the owners of a carnival named La Vida Funland . Her father had died from a brain tumor , causing devastation to her family . Her mother Shirley had to remain independent for the children . As Shirley manages the carnival , she encountered some problems such as accidents in which one of her employees died . It was then believed that the husband of the employees went into their house , causing arson which apparently have accidentally made the girl comatose .
