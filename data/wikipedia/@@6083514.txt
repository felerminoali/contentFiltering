Fitch received a title shot against the UFC Welterweight champion , Georges St-Pierre , on August 9 , 2008. # Background # After graduating from Carroll High School ( Fort Wayne , Indiana ) , Fitch enrolled in at Purdue University in 1997 and graduated in 2002 with a bachelor 's in physical education and a minor in history . Fitch secured a starting spot early on in his career , but struggled to find initial success .
