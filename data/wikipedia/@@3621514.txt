It is a joint collaboration between Ishimori Productions and Toei . The series was broadcast on TV Asahi . The first episode aired on January 29 , 2006 , and with the final episode airing on January 21 , 2007 , completing the series with 49 episodes . The series represents the 35th anniversary of the Kamen Rider Series , as indicated by a notice at the beginning of the pilot episode reading , in Japanese , Kamen Rider 35th Anniversary Production . ' ' Kamen Rider Kabuto ' ' is the first Kamen Rider Series to be broadcast in high-definition format .
