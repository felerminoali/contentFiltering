Rasa von Werder ( born Rosa Sofia Jakstas , better-known as Kellie Everts ) is a former stripper , female bodybuilder , and founder of her own church . # Early life # Everts was born on July 16 , 1945 in Calw , 33 km west of Stuttgart , the state capital of Baden-Wurttemburg in West Germany . Her family had fled from Lithuania to escape the Communist take-over . In June 1949 , Rasa and her family moved to the United States and when she was eight years old they settled on a farm in New Jersey . # The Stripper for God # Blessed with impressive features , Everts won the title of Miss Nude Universe in July 1967 and Miss Body Beautiful in 1974 . She later made nine appearances ( with three articles ) in ' ' Playboy ' ' , and became an exotic dancer . In 1973 , Everts came up with the idea a stripper could also be a minister .
