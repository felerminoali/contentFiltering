The 2011 Manhattan terrorism plot was alleged to be an alleged conspiracy by two Muslim Arab-Americans to bomb various targets in the Manhattan borough of New York City , New York , USA . It was alleged that they planned to attack an unspecified synagogue and one of them expressed interest in blowing up a church and the Empire State Building . New York City law enforcement arrested the two suspects , 26-year-old Ahmed Ferhani and 20-year-old Mohamed Mamdouh , in a sting operation on May 11 , 2011 .
