' ' Fora de um Desejo ' ' is a Brazilian telenovela produced by Globo TV and was shown in the schedule of 18 hours between May 10 , 1999 and January 29 , 2000 , in 226 chapters . It was written by Gilberto Braga and as collaborators with Alcides Nogueira Marques , Lilian Garcia , Eliane Garcia , Philip Miguez and Mark Silver and directed by Mauro Mendona Filho , Carlos Arajo , Fabricio Mamberti and Joo Camargo , with production of the core Marcos Paulo .
