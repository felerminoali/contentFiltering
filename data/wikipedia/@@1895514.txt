birthplace =Bognor Regis , England Bruce Cripps OBE ( born 2 November 1941 , Bognor Regis , Sussex , England ) known by his stage name Bruce Welch is an English guitarist , songwriter , producer and singer , best known as a member of The Shadows . # Biography # His parents ( Stan Cripps and Grace Welch ) relocated him to 15 Broadwood View , Chester-le-Street : she died when Welch was aged 6 . Welch grew up with his Aunt Sadie in Chester-le-Street , County Durham .
