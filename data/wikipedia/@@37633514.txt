The 2013 NASCAR Nationwide Series was the 32nd season of semi-professional stock car racing in the United States . The season was contested over thirty-three races and started on February 23 , 2013 at Daytona International Speedway , with the DRIVE4COPD 300 , and ended on November 16 with the Ford EcoBoost 300 at Homestead-Miami Speedway . Austin Dillon of Richard Childress Racing won the Drivers ' Championship , becoming the first driver in the three major NASCAR series to do so without recording a win .
