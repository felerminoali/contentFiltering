KCSP ( 610 AM , 610 Sports ) is a sports/talk radio station located in Kansas City , Missouri . The Entercom-owned station broadcasts on 610 kHz . From March 31 , 2011 to March 5 , 2012 its programming was simulcast on KMBZ-FM 's 98.1-HD2 subchannel . Its transmitter is located in Prairie Village , Kansas , and studios are located in Mission , Kansas . KCSP AM is a class B regional station , with a broadcasting power of 5,000 Watts in both the daytime and nighttime , using a non-directional antenna ( 1 tower ) . Although the station had the slogan The Football Channel when it began in June 2003 , it is currently the flagship station of a baseball team , the Kansas City Royals , whose rights it reacquired for the Entercom radio network in 2008 which had held the rights on KMBZ before .
