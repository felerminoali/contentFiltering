' ' The Hocking Hills is a deeply dissected area of the Allegheny Plateau in Ohio , primarily in Hocking County , that features cliffs , gorges , rock shelters , and waterfalls . The relatively extreme topography in this area is due to the Blackhand Sandstone ( so named because of Native American graphics on the formation near Newark , Ohio ) , a particular formation that is thick , hard and weather-resistant , and so forms high cliffs and narrow , deep gorges .
