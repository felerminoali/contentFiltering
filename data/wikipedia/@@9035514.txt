birthplace = Rio de Janeiro , Rio de Janeiro , Brazil Marlia Pra ( born on January 22 , 1943 in Rio de Janeiro , Rio de Janeiro ) is a Brazilian actress . Hailed as one of the decade 's ( 1980 's ) ten best actresses by Pauline Kael , Pra won the National Society of Film Critics Award for Best Actress in 1982 for her role in Hector Babenco 's acclaimed Pixote , and has also received Best Actress awards at the Gramado Film Festival ( Triple Award Winner ) and at the Cartegena Film Festival for Carlos Diegues ' Better Days Ahead . Her other films include Bar Esperanza , Paul Morrissey 's Mixed Blood , Angels of the Night , The Interview , and Carlos Diegues ' Tieta . # Biography # Pra was born to actors Manuel Pra and Dinorah Marzullo . Her sister is also an accomplished actress Sandra Pra . She is the mother of actor Ricardo Graa Mello , and actresses Esperana Motta and Nina Morena .
