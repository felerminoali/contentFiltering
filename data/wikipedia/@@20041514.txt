Dr. Lisa M. Schenck , J.S.D. , is an American attorney and academic . In November 2009 , Dr. Schenck was appointed as the Associate Dean for Academic Affairs at The George Washington University Law School . In this position , she manages the . The Law School has over 320 adjunct professors who teach the 2,000 students studying in its Juris Doctor ( J.D. ) and Master of Laws ( LL.M. ) degree programs . In March 2010 , Dr. Schenck was appointed as a Professorial Lecturer in Law , and teaches Military Justice . She joined the Law School in 2009 after serving in the United States Army Judge Advocate General 's Corps for more than 25 years . Established in 1865 , The George Washington University Law School is the oldest law school in the District of Columbia .
