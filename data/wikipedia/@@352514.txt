He is the father of actor Josh Brolin and husband of singer/actress Barbra Streisand. # Early life # Brolin was born Craig Kenneth Bruderlin in Los Angeles , California . The eldest of two brothers and two sisters , he was the son of Helen Sue ( ne Mansur ) , a housewife , and Henry Hurst Bruderlin , a building contractor . The family settled in Westwood after his birth . As a young child , he was apparently more interested in animals and airplanes than in acting . When Brolin was 10 in 1950 , he began building and flying model airplanes . As a teenaged filmgoer in the mid-1950s he was particularly fascinated with actor James Dean .
