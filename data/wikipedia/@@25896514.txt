The episode was aired on April 13 , 2010 on ABC in the United States . The episode was written by executive producers Edward Kitsis &amp; Adam Horowitz and directed by Dan Attias , who previously directed the first season episode Numbers . The title alludes to the season two episode Everybody Hates Hugo .
