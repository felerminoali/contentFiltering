The 2014 Virginia Tech Hokies baseball team is representing Virginia Tech in the 2014 NCAA Division I baseball season . Head Coach Patrick Mason is in his 1st year coaching the Hokies . They are coming off a 2013 season , in which they had a 40 win season . 15 of them came in the ACC .
